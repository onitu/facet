•  What is Onitu ?

    Onitu is a free, secure, self hosted, customisable file sharing platform. With Onitu, you can access, share, upload your files from anywhere to your personal space.

  •  How much does subscription cost ?

    Onitu is completely free of charge. Not only is it free, but it is also open-source (more info about that here).

  •  Can I run it on any device I want ?

    The currently supported systems are Windows, Mac and Linux.

  •  What file sharing services will be synchronized ?

    You can gather your files altogether from several sources, also known as "drivers". The supported drivers are:

    • Dropbox
    • Amazon S3
    • SSH
    • FTP
    • Local
